Origami Fabric Wallets

  • Home
  • Origami Fabric Wallets

Inspired by the traditional Japanese art of paper folding dating back to the 17th Century, these origami folded wallets are handcrafted from folded fabric and reinforced with adhesives for increased durability. Each wallet has pockets for cash as well as folds created for holding cards.

Approximately 4”x 4” when closed and 8”x4” when open. These wallets make a beautiful yet practical gift. There are four different patterns to choose from:

Purple
Vivid Purple with Blue Flower Pattern

Navy Blue
Navy Blue with Blue & White Geometric Pattern

Dark Green
Dark Green with Bright Green Floral Pattern

Moss Green
Moss Green with White Paisley Pattern
