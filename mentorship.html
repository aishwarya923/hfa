Mentorship Program

  • Home
  • Mentorship Program

One of the many exciting developments following the Women Empowerment Project is the formal launch of a mentorship program to support the new micro enterprise cooperatives (SHGs) formed by project graduates. Inspired by the success of long-term, international volunteer workshops like the Secret Pillow Project and the Made with Love Italian cushions, AMMACHI Labs will soon be inviting successful women entrepreneurs from around the world to connect with a specific cooperative and spend a year mentoring the women through hands on workshops and skill training.


Mentorship programs in Entrepreneurship, Product Development, and Personality Development (LEE Skills) will be enacted. A full support staff, both within and outside AMMACHI Labs, will assist the mentor.

After the initial success of one of the WE Projectâ€™s first international collaborations- the Secret Pillow Project, our London-based partner decided to do a major scale-up. In April, 2014, she started a crowd-funding Kickstarter campaign to raise money for 500 pillows to be made by our WE Project cooperatives. The campaign was a great success and created sustainable revenue streams for our cooperatives.
