Handpainted Murals

  • Home
  • Handpainted Murals

These beautiful handpainted murals were inspired by the ancient traditional art of Kerala most often found on the walls of temples, churches and palaces The origins of this technique which dates back to the 9th century CE.Following in that tradition, these pieces are meticulously hand-painted utilising ancient principles and techniques. Each stroke is masterfully applied by either single or teams of artisans working meticulously on each piece of art. The stunning results speak for themself!

There are three murals to choose from, a traditional Lord Ganesha, a beautiful Devi Goddess and a stunning scene of the Divine Flute Player: Lord Krishna.
